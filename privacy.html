Privacy Policy

Applies to: BagPipes TikTok Auto (Windows desktop app)
Effective date: June 29, 2026

Overview

BagPipes TikTok Auto is a Windows desktop application that crops horizontal video clips to 9:16 vertical format and uploads them to TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

TikTok permissions requested

The app requests the following TikTok OAuth scopes when you authorize it:

  • video.upload — to upload video files to your TikTok account.
  • video.publish — to publish uploaded videos as posts on your TikTok account.

The app does not access your TikTok profile, follower list, messages, comments, analytics, or any other account data beyond what is needed to upload and publish videos on your behalf.

How your data is stored

After you authorize the app, TikTok issues an access token and a refresh token. These are stored exclusively on your local PC at:

%APPDATA%\BSTikTokAuto\tokens.json

These tokens are never transmitted to any server, database, or third party controlled by the developer. They exist solely on your machine and are used only to authenticate API calls made directly from the app to TikTok's servers.

App preferences are stored locally at:

%APPDATA%\BSTikTokAuto\settings.json

These settings contain no personal information.

Video files

The app processes video files that you select. It crops and encodes them locally using ffmpeg and stores the output temporarily at:

%APPDATA%\BSTikTokAuto\processed\

Processed videos are uploaded directly to TikTok's API from your machine. The developer does not receive copies of your video files.

Third-party services

The app communicates only with TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com and www.tiktok.com). No analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting services are included.

Your rights

  • Revoke access — Revoke the app's TikTok authorization at any time from your TikTok account under Settings & Privacy → Security → Authorized Apps.
  • Delete local data — Delete %APPDATA%\BSTikTokAuto\ to remove all locally stored tokens and settings.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
